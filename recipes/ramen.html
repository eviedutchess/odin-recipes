Ramen

Description

The word 'ramen' means 'pulled noodles'.

South Korea has the highest per capita instant noodle consumption worldwide at 74.1 servings of instant noodles per person.

Ingredients

  • ramen pack
  • water
  • eggs
  • green onions

Steps

  1. Boil water.
  2. Add noodles and seasoning.
  3. Add a boiled egg and chopped green onions.
